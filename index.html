Maneyangala

Tucked away amidst the rolling farmlands and untouched beauty of Dakshina Karnataka, Maneyangala offers an unparalleled retreat for those seeking peace, privacy, and a deeper connection with nature. Built with traditional red-stone and clay tile architecture, our retreat offers a cool, earthy haven where nature’s rhythm is your soundtrack. Whether gathering with loved ones or hosting a memorable event, you’ll find the space to reconnect with each other and the nature around you.

Our Story

From Debits and Credits to Dawns and Dusks, we're Sachin and Ranjana, two Chartered Accountants who fell in love with rural Karnataka. The pandemic sparked a creative awakening, inspiring us to share this haven with others. Thus, our homestay and event space was born.

When we were creating this space, we wanted a name that reflected the heart of our vision. In the local Kannada language, "maney" signifies a house, a place of warmth and shelter, while "angala" evokes the image of a spacious yard, a place for gathering and connection. Together, "Maneyangala" reflects our vision of a welcoming home with an open, inviting space – a place where you can truly feel at ease.
